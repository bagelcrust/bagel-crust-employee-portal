BAGEL CRUST BUSINESS RULES
==========================

COLOR SYSTEM
------------
RED    = Closed (0 hours)
YELLOW = Limited hours (under 60 hours/day)
GREEN  = Full hours (60+ hours/day)


RED - CLOSED
------------
- New Year's Day (Jan 1)
- Christmas Day (Dec 25)


YELLOW - LIMITED HOURS
----------------------
Holidays:
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Eve (Dec 24)
- New Year's Eve (Dec 31)
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (Jul 4)
- Juneteenth (Jun 19)
- Labor Day

Seasonal:
- Summer (mid-May to mid-August)
  Start: Around May 12 (after graduation)
  End: Around Aug 22 (move-in week)

- Spring Break (early March, ~1 week)
- Thanksgiving Break (Nov 25-27)
- Winter Break (Dec 23 onward)


GREEN - FULL HOURS
------------------
Regular Operations:
- Spring semester (Jan 8 - Apr 26)
- Fall semester (Aug 26 - Dec 6)

Peak Days (extra busy):
- Football home games (Sept-Nov Saturdays)
- Homecoming weekend
- THON weekend (February)
- Move-in week (late August)
- Graduation days
- Arts Fest (mid-July spike during summer)


WEEKLY PATTERN
--------------
Busiest: Saturday, Sunday
Slowest: Monday, Tuesday


SHIFT PATTERN
-------------
Most shifts start 5-8 AM (morning operation)
